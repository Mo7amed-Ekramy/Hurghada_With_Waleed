Giftun Island (Mahmya & National Park)

🚤 Boat Trip 🐠 Snorkeling 🏖️ Clear Water

Most programs include lunch, drinks, and equipment on board, plus free hotel pickup. Prices for foreign guests run from about $22 to $40 depending on the boat and stops. Egyptian guests get special local rates—ask Walid on WhatsApp. Ideal for families and couples.
